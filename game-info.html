Stewardship Life

This build follows David Strege’s vision: steward time, talents, and treasures. Plans come first; events test them.

  • Classroom: set job, spend/save/share, weekly time, talents, insurance — then see a lifetime projection and teacher debrief notes.
  • Ongoing: live year by year from 25→85. Adjust your plan. Face short-term temptations vs serving others, plus real setbacks.
  • Score: Wealth × (1 + Health% + Happiness%). Money alone is not the win.
  • Wisdom pattern: spend less than you earn, share ≥10%, invest the rest, rest, serve, seek counsel, limit debt and get-rich schemes.
